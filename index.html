Japanese Hiragana & Romaji Matching Game

As both a student and a teacher, I’m always looking for different ways to study. Using what I learned with JavaScript and CSS, I developed this memory matching game for students learning Japanese. Users match Japanese hiragana characters to the English phonetic equivalent, called romaji. This app can easily be adapted to new study materials, such as vocabulary or scientific formulas.

Local Weather App

A simple weather app that makes an API call and displays local weather to the user. Based on the current weather, the background, icon, and message are conditionally rendered to the user. This was the first app that I developed on my own outside of a code-along tutorial course. There are many paths for improvement in the future, such as multi-day forecast results or adding the ability to search for weather in other cities.
